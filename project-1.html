Project Overview

Secondary students frequently write numerous essays, speeches, and articles. Unfortunately, many of these valuable works go undocumented, depriving other students of potential benefits. To address this, I developed this app to empower students to share, read, and write essays, articles, and stories.

It is going live very soon. Check out the demo and code here.
